## 🚀 What I'm Building

### 🌐 [Nyamai Nexus Group](https://github.com/derricknyamai11/nyamai-nexus)
Africa's premier AI consulting, automation, data engineering, ERP, talent marketplace, and professional writing firm. A Next.js 14 monorepo featuring a public marketing website with Sanity CMS and an invitation-only client portal built with Clerk auth and Supabase — serving businesses across 6 service lines and 3 SaaS products.

### 📄 [DocInt Africa](https://github.com/derricknyamai11/docint-africa)
A document intelligence SaaS for African law firms and SMEs. Provides authenticated organisation workspaces, document uploads, semantic search, contract date alerts, and team settings. Built with FastAPI, MongoDB, React, and Tailwind — with optional email and WhatsApp integrations.

### 🏪 [NyamaiTechHub Marketplace](https://github.com/derricknyamai11/NyamaiTechHub-Marketplace)
A full-stack freelance marketplace connecting global talent with opportunities. Built with React 19, Node.js/Express, SQLite, and Socket.IO — with multi-currency payment support including M-Pesa, Stripe, and PayPal.

### 🛒 [Sokoni ERP](https://github.com/derricknyamai11/Sokoni-erp)
A full-stack ERP for East African retail and distribution SMEs — combining point-of-sale, inventory, accounting, and compliance in one platform. Features multi-user support, role-based access, and sandbox-ready M-Pesa and KRA eTIMS integrations.

### 💊 Careloop
A hyperlocal health resource platform featuring **MamaAI** — an AI assistant for maternal and community health. Bridging the healthcare access gap in underserved communities.

### 🚚 Sambua App (SambuaGo / Njenje)
A food delivery marketplace targeting Kenya's coastal counties — connecting local restaurants and vendors with customers through a fast, mobile-first experience.
